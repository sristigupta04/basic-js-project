Pizza Recipes

  • Prep Time: 30 mins
  • Cook Time: 30 mins
  • Rise Time: 90 mins
  • Total Time: 2 hrs 30 mins
  • Servings: 4–6
  • Yield: Two 10-inch pizzas

For the Pizza Dough

  • 1½ cups warm water (105–115°F)
  • 2¼ tsp active dry yeast
  • 3¾ cups bread flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp sugar

Toppings (Choose Any)

Tomato sauce, mozzarella, parmesan, mushrooms, peppers, olives, basil, arugula, pesto, pepperoni, onions, ham, or sausage.

Steps

  1. Mix water, yeast, and sugar; rest 5 mins.
  2. Add flour, salt, and oil; knead 7–10 mins.
  3. Let rise for 1½ hrs; divide into 2 balls.
  4. Roll out, top as desired.
  5. Bake at 475°F (245°C) for 10–15 mins until golden.
