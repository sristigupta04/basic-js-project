Pizza Recipes

  • Prep Time: 30 mins
  • Cook Time: 30 mins
  • Rise Time: 90 mins
  • Total Time: 2 hrs 30 mins
  • Servings: 4–6
  • Yield: Two 10-inch pizzas

For the Pizza Dough

  • 1½ cups warm water (105–115°F)
  • 2¼ tsp active dry yeast
  • 3¾ cups bread flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp sugar

Avocado Toast

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 1–2

Mash a ripe avocado and spread it over toasted bread. Add a squeeze of lime juice, salt, and pepper. Top with chili flakes or diced tomatoes if desired.

Tuna and Avocado Salad

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 1–2

Mix drained tuna with chopped avocado, a little mayo, mustard, salt, and pepper. Serve in a wrap, with crackers, or enjoy on its own.

Fruit Plate with Dip

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 2–4

Arrange assorted fresh fruits on a plate. Mix plain yogurt with honey, vanilla, and cinnamon for a quick, tasty dip.

Cheese and Cracker Platter

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 2–4

Arrange a mix of cheeses and crackers on a plate. Add nuts or dried fruits for extra flavor and crunch.

Sandwich / Wrap

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 1–2

Use bread or a tortilla and fill with deli meat, cheese, lettuce, tomato, avocado, or a tuna-mayo mix. A classic, easy meal you can customize any way you like.
